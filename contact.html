Frequently Asked Questions

How long until I get a reply?

We aim to respond to all messages within 24–48 business hours. High-volume periods may take slightly longer, but we read every message and respond to each one personally.

Can I request a new tool?

Absolutely! We love building tools our users actually need. Select "Feature Request" as the subject and describe the tool you'd like to see. Many of our current tools were added based on user suggestions.

I found a bug — what info should I include?

To help us fix it quickly, please include: (1) your browser name and version (e.g., Chrome 122), (2) your operating system (e.g., Windows 11), and (3) the step-by-step actions that triggered the bug. A screenshot is also very helpful if possible.
