📊 2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly

We do not require you to provide personal information to use our tools. However, if you contact us (e.g., via email), we may collect your name and email address to respond to your inquiry.

B. Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, certain information is collected automatically through browser technology and third-party services:

  • Log Data: Your browser may send information such as your IP address, browser type and version, pages visited, time and date of visit, and time spent on pages. This is standard server log data managed by Blogger/Google's infrastructure.
  • Device Information: General information about the device used to access the site (screen size, operating system, browser) may be collected by analytics and advertising services.
  • Usage Data: Aggregated, anonymous data about which tools are used most frequently, to help us improve the Service.
  • Cookies: Small data files placed on your device. See Section 4 for details.

C. What We Do NOT Collect

  • The contents of files you process with our tools (PDFs, images, documents)
  • Passwords you generate using our password tool
  • Text you enter into our text tools
  • Any output produced by our tools

⚙️ 3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and maintain the Service
  • Understand how users interact with our tools (aggregate analytics only)
  • Improve the website's performance, features, and user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Respond to your communications if you contact us

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

🛡️ 7. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We implement the following measures:

  • Client-Side Processing: All file conversions and tool operations occur locally in your browser, meaning your documents, images, and files are never transmitted over the internet to our servers.
  • HTTPS: Our website is served over HTTPS, encrypting data in transit between your browser and our hosting platform.
  • No Data Storage: We do not maintain databases of user files, outputs, or personal information.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect any information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

🌍 10. GDPR (EU/UK) & CCPA (California)

For EU/UK Residents — General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

If you are located in the European Union or United Kingdom, you have specific rights under the GDPR, including rights to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection. Our legal bases for processing data include:

  • Legitimate Interests: Analytics and service improvement.
  • Consent: Personalized advertising (you can withdraw consent at any time).
  • Legal Obligation: Where required by law.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority (e.g., the ICO in the UK, or your country's Data Protection Authority).

For California Residents — California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) / CPRA:

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected and how it is used and shared.
  • Delete personal information we have collected (subject to certain exceptions).
  • Opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell personal information.
  • Non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights.

To submit a request, contact us at the email in Section 12 with the subject "CCPA/Privacy Request."
